Classical Encryption Methods

Caesar Cipher

One of the oldest and simplest encryption techniques. Each letter in the plaintext is shifted by a fixed number of positions.

Vigenère Cipher

A polyalphabetic cipher that uses a keyword to determine the shift of each letter, making it harder to break than Caesar.
